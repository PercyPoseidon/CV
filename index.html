I am a lecturer and a graduate at the Institute of Learning Sciences and Technologies, National Tsing Hua University (NTHU), Taiwan. Previously, I graduated from The University of Sydney (USyd) with a master's degree in Interaction Design and Electronic Arts in 2019. My research interests are learner experience research, language learning, empathy and reading. My research inspiration is to entitle users to autonomy, motivation and improvement while interacting or using a tangible and intangible service or device.
